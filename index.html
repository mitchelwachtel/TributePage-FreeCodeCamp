Quick Bio

  • Born: Oct. 9, 1967
  • First Match: 1987
  • Wrestled for NJPW, ECW, and WCW throughout the 90s
  • Joined WWF (WWE) in 2000
  • Won the WWE title in 2004
  • Denied custody of his son, Dominic by Rey Mysterio at SummerSlam 2005
  • Died: Nov. 13, 2005 (38)
